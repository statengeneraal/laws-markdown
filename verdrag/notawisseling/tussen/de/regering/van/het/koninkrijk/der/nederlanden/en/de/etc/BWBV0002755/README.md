##Notawisseling tussen de Regering van het Koninkrijk der Nederlanden en de Regering van de Unie van Socialistische Sowjet-Republieken betreffende luchtdiensten

### 1  

As from April 1, 1984, Aeroflot will be allowed to overfly Netherlands territory without landing or with a technical landing with any number of scheduled flights to/from points in Europe and to/from points beyond in USA, Canada, Mexico, Jamaica, Nicaragua, Cuba, Peru, Bolivia, Brazil, Venezuela, Panama, Columbia with any type of subsonic aircraft.  

### 2  

As from the same date KLM will be allowed to overfly USSR territory through the Tashkent route without landing or with a technical landing with any number of scheduled flights to/from points in Pakistan, India, Maldives, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Australia, New Zealand, Philippines, Hong Kong with any type of subsonic aircraft.  

### 3  

Additional points beyond in other countries than listed under 1 and 2 above may be established between the aeronautical authorities of both Parties.  

### 4  

Requests by Aeroflot and KLM for overflying with their non-scheduled flights without landing or with a technical landing in the territory of the other party along the routes meant in I and 2 will be favourably considered by the respective authorities.
